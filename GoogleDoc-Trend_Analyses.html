PyCity Schools Analysis

From studying the results of our data analysis, there are a number of observable trends regarding academic performance in our district.

  • Students of charter schools significantly outperformed their district school counterparts.

  • The total number of students at a particular school has an inverse relationship to academic performance.

  • The budget set aside per student had little effect on academic performance.

As a result of these observations, policy recommendations include promoting a low student to teacher ratio rather than an increase in school funding. Some socio-economic factors which may affect our findings were not studied (avg parent income, district location, etc.).
